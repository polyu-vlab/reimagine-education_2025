About the Project

GPTutor is a Generative AI-powered Intelligent Tutoring System developed at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. It aims to revolutionize education by providing personalized, interactive, and engaging learning experiences for students across various disciplines.

The platform integrates advanced AI technologies to support self-directed learning, instant feedback, and immersive simulation-based activities. By bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, GPTutor empowers students to become career-ready professionals.

Our Mission

To empower students and educators with cutting-edge AI tools that enhance learning efficiency, foster curiosity, and promote lifelong learning. GPTutor is designed to be accessible, reliable, and adaptable to the evolving needs of modern education.
